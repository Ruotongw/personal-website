Purpose of the Study

This study aims to understand people's experiences with keeping track of information from remote meetings. Through analysis of survey responses, we hope to identify new opportunities for designing tools that could create better remote meeting experiences.

Study Procedures

Our survey asks about your experience of keeping track of information from remote meetings and is hosted on Qualtrics and Prolific.

Benefits

You may reflect on your experience of keeping track of information from remote meetings. Your answers will contribute to research on building tools and technologies for better meeting experiences.

Risks

We foresee extremely minimal risks with this study.
